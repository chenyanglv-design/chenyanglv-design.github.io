Life Church Digital Generosity

The giving feature on the Life Church app is where people begin their generosity journey and grow their relationship with God. It is very important for Life Church because 90% of the gifts that Life Church receives every year come from it.

I have been working as the Design Lead on the giving feature since August 2021, and my goal has always been to provide a frictionless giving experience for people who feel a passion to trust God financially!

Solutions

To reduce the cognitive burden of completing the giving form, the information hierarchy was reorganized. Relevant selectors were combined and brought closer together, while less important selectors were deemphasized.
Simplify the process to complete the giving form:

1. Automatically set the location for users when they enter the Geofence of a Life Church location.
2. Provide more information for different funds to help them make a decision.
3. Simplify the process of adding a payment method.
4. Enable users to give without an account using Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Motivate users to be more generous through revealing them the impact that their gifts have made at through Life.Church.
